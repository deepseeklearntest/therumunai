# Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Therumunai accepts contributions under a **Contributor License Agreement (CLA)**. The CLA
keeps the public project open under [AGPL-3.0](../LICENSE) while granting the project
**Steward** (see [`GOVERNANCE.md`](../GOVERNANCE.md)) the rights needed to sustain,
relicense, or transfer the project if that ever becomes necessary.

**Every contributor must agree to this CLA before their pull request can be merged.** This
is enforced automatically — see [§ How signing works](#how-signing-works).

> **Why a CLA?** Without it, each contributor retains copyright over their commits, and the
> project could never be relicensed, dual-licensed, or transferred without tracking down
> every past contributor. The CLA is what keeps the project's future flexible while the
> public codebase stays open. It does **not** take your rights away — you keep full
> ownership of your contribution (see § 2).

  who maintain the Project.
- **"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship — including any modifications
  or additions to existing work — that You intentionally submit to the Project (e.g., via
  pull request, patch, or issue attachment).

### 2. You retain ownership

You retain all right, title, and interest in Your Contributions. This CLA does not transfer
ownership; it grants the licenses described below.

- **Individuals** agree on their own behalf.

  entity's behalf are then covered.

## How signing works

1. Open your pull request as normal.
2. A **CLA assistant bot** will comment with a one-time link. Signing records your GitHub
   identity against this agreement.
3. The **CLA status check** must be green before a maintainer can merge.
4. You only sign **once** — it covers all your future contributions.

> Maintainers: the CLA check is intended to be wired up via the
> [CLA Assistant](https://github.com/cla-assistant/cla-assistant) GitHub app (or
> equivalent) and added as a **required status check** in branch protection — see
> [`docs/BRANCH_PROTECTION.md`](./BRANCH_PROTECTION.md). Until the app is installed, the
> Steward collects CLA agreement manually before merging.
